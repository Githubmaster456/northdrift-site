Our Interior Packages

Basic Package

Quick vacuuming of carpets and seats, wipe-down of dash, console, door panels, and light cleaning of windows. This is ideal for regular upkeep or quick refreshes. No shampooing or deep stain removal.

Full Package

Deep vacuuming of the entire car, soap and scrub mats, thorough wipe-down of dashboard, vents, door panels, shampooed fabric seats and shampooed all carpet. Leather is gently cleaned and conditioned, and all glass surfaces are polished streak-free. Not a single spot on the car missed if there is contact us and we will come back and fix it for free!!

Note: Vehicles with excessive pet hair or heavy soil may incur an additional $50 charge due to time required. We’ll provide an accurate estimate before service.
