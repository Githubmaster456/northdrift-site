Interior Detail

Our Interior Detailing service offers two packages depending on your needs: A light detail including quick vacuuming of carpets and seats, wipe-down of dash, console, door panels, and light cleaning of windows. No shampooing or deep stain removal.

Our Full Detail service begins with a deep vacuuming of all upholstery and carpeted areas. We steam clean floor mats, wipe down every surface including dashboard, vents, and door panels, and shampoo fabric seats. We’ll make your interior look like the day it came off the lot. Leather is gently cleaned and conditioned, and all glass surfaces are polished streak-free.

Please note: Vehicles with excessive pet hair or heavy soil may incur an additional $50 charge due to the extra time and attention required. We’ll always do our best to provide an accurate estimate before beginning service.
