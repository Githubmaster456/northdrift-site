Our Exterior Packages

Basic Package

Our Basic Exterior Detailing includes a hand wash, wheel and tire scrub, streak-free glass cleaning, and application of a premium wax sealant. This is ideal for regular upkeep and improving your vehicle’s shine.

Full Package

Full Exterior includes pre-soak, foam bath, clay bar decontamination, deep wheel and tire treatment, trim polish, and a professional-grade ceramic or hydrophobic wax sealant for long-lasting protection and gloss.

Paint Correction

Paint Correction includes our Full Detail plus a multi-stage polish to remove surface swirls, oxidation, and minor scratches, restoring true depth and clarity to your paint finish.
